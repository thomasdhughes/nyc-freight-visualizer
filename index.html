Visualizing Freight Flows in New York City

Created by Thomas Hughes

with data from Davis III et al., 2023


Every day, 350,000 freight trucks traverse the city, making deliveries at all hours of the day and night.

This map visualizes this truck activity, revealing the hidden rhythms of urban logistics.

Steel Arteries is a visualization of freight truck activity in the Big Apple, built on the synthetic dataset created in the 2023 paper "Quantifying and Visualizing City Truck Route Network Efficiency Using a Virtual Testbed" by Davis III et al. These are the trucks and the drivers that make the city run.

To the original researchers: thank you for creating such a rich dataset, and thank you for sharing it with the world!

Tips:
  • Click any neighborhood to see how many trucks are driving through it
  • Click the any industry drop-down to see the flow of freight trucks for a specific industry
  • Click and drag the timeline to change the time of day
